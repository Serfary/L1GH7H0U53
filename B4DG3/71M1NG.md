# 71M1NG
## Overview
Did you ever play "Red Light, Green Light" as a kid? Now's your chance to put those reaction skills to the test.

## Details
- 5 stages of difficulty (1200ms, 990ms, 660ms, 440ms, 220ms)
- Green appears at random intervals between 1-3 seconds

## Testing Notes
- Red vs Green LED
- Left vs Right Button
- Button Press vs Release
